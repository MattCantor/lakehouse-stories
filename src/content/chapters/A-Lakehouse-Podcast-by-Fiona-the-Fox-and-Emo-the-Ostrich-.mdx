Episode One: Welcome to A Lakehouse Podcast!
Fiona: My name is Fiona the Fox, and welcome to the new podcast I’m making with Emo the Ostrich! Oh my god, I can’t believe that we’re actually making one!
Emo: This is going to rule!
F: So, Emo, we’ve never actually worked together on anything at school.
E: Maybe that’s because we’ve never been in the same class?
F: Yeah. I’m so excited to work on this with you.
E: Well, Fiona, I guess we should tell the listeners what this podcast is going to be about?
F: Yeah. Well, Emo and I are going to have a different guest star most of the time. We’ll talk to them. But other times we will have no guest stars at all, and we’ll talk about our own topics.
E: I have some very good ideas.
F: No spoilers, Emo! Don’t make this a repeat of our play.
E: A play that should be one of the topics.
F: Kind of a spoiler, Emo.
E: Is that what a spoiler is?
F: Yeah..oh boy.
E: Spoilers are a social construct made to dim down our own insecurities!
F: What?
E: What?
F: Nothing. Well, anyway, I hope you guys tune in to our new podcast!
